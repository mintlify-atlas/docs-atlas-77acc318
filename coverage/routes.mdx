Deltarune features branching routes that lead to significantly different scenes, dialogue, and outcomes. Understanding which routes are covered is important for knowing what content the dataset actually includes.

## Deltarune's route structure

Deltarune has three main route types across its released chapters:

- **Normal Route** — The standard playthrough path, available in all chapters.
- **Snowgrave Route / Weird Route** — An alternate dark path available in Chapters 2 and 4, involving different choices and substantially different content.
- **Sword Route** — An alternate path in Chapter 3 with different scenes and outcomes.

## Chapter 3 Sword Route

Chapter 3 is the only chapter in this dataset with alternate route content included. The Sword Route — which involves different story beats and scenes compared to the Normal Route — is fully transcribed alongside the Normal Route content.

This makes Chapter 3 the most complete chapter in the dataset from a route coverage perspective.

## Missing: Snowgrave/Weird Route

<Warning>
  The Snowgrave Route (Chapters 2 and 4) is entirely absent from this dataset. No Snowgrave/Weird Route dialogue, scenes, or content of any kind is included.
</Warning>

The Snowgrave Route represents a distinct and substantial alternate playthrough. If you are training a model or building a system that needs to handle Snowgrave Route content — such as understanding specific character behavior, alternate dialogue, or route-specific plot points — this dataset will not cover those cases.

## Implications for model training

If you are using this dataset for model training or retrieval, keep the following in mind:

- **Normal Route only users:** Chapters 1–4 Normal Route content is fully covered (with Chapter 4 at Beta quality).
- **Completionist/all-routes users:** Chapter 3 Sword Route is included, but Snowgrave Route content for Chapters 2 and 4 is missing. A model trained on this dataset will not be able to reliably answer questions about Snowgrave Route events.
- **Chapter-specific filtering:** Because each chapter is a separate file, you can load only the chapters whose coverage meets your requirements.
